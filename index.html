Safebag System

"SafeBag" is an innovative and smart system for baggage management and safety at airports, utilizing advanced technologies like RFID and NFC. The system leverages the NFC feature in the Saudi passport to verify the traveler's identity and baggage ownership, enhancing security and ensuring real-time tracking of bags throughout the journey. This system aims to reduce financial losses for airlines and improve the traveler experience by ensuring secure baggage delivery. It also contributes to the development of Saudi airports, boosting their technological capabilities in line with Vision 2030.
